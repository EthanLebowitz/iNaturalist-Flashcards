About

This project is essentially crowd sourced tracking flashcards. All cards are generated from iNaturalist observations. All observations used to generate cards have "research grade" identifications (meaning a critical mass of identifiers have aggreed on an identification) assuring for some degree of recognizability. Clicking Show Answer will reveal iNaturalist's identification of the tracks. I can't guarantee that 100% of these will be right.

Some cards will have more than one image associated with them. These can be navigated using the arrows, or the arrow buttons on your keyboard, or by swiping on mobile. To constrain cards to your region, use the filters in the More menu.

If you like this site, check out my other projects or get in touch at ethleb.com.

I hope you enjoy!

Nature Flashcards

These are flashcards generated from research grade observations on iNaturalist. Try to identify the species using the location and images, and then click "Show Answer" to see if you were right. If you get it wrong, click "Missed it", and you will be given the card again later in the session.
