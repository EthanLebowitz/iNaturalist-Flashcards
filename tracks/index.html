Track and Sign Identification Flashcards

These are track and sign flashcards generated from research grade iNaturalist observations for practicing identification of tracks. For most decks there are hundreds of thousands or millions such observations, so it's like an infinite track and sign quiz!

An observation being classified as "research grade" means that it has been agreed on by a critical mass of identifiers on iNaturalist. This helps ensure some quality, but since all IDs are done by the community there may be some mistakes.

If you like this site, check out my other projects or get in touch at ethleb.com. Also, this site is open source, and you can view the code or contribute on github!

I hope you enjoy!

Ethan Lebowitz

Species Flashcards

These are flashcards generated from research grade observations on iNaturalist. Try to identify the species using the location and images, and then click "Show Answer" to see if you were right. If you get it wrong, click "Missed it", and you will be given the card again later in the session.
